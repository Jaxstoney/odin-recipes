Annies Macaroni

Description

Made with organic pasta, shells and real aged cheddar. You need milk a colander and something to stir with.

  1. BOIL 6 cups of water in a medium saucepan.
  2. Stir in PASTA and cook 8-10 minutes or until done, stirring occasionally.
  3. Drain pasta in COLANDER. while pasta is draining.
  4. Add 3 Tbsp low fat MILK to the warm saucepan. Richer flavor: add 2 Tbsp unsalted butter.
  5. SPRINKLE cheese over milk. Stir to combine
  6. Add cooked pasta and STIR well.
