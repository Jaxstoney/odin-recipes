Annies Macaroni

Description

Made with organic pasta, shells and real aged cheddar. You need milk a colander and something to stir with.

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a saucepan, add noodles and cook for 3 minutes hile stiring occasionally
  2. Turn off heat, add contents of flavor packet, stir well for 1 minute until flavorings is fully disolved. Makes two 8 oz servings.
  3. Product is very hot. Allow to cool, serve and enjoy.
